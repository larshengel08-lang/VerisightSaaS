# Loep Culture Assessment Follow-On Outcome Register

**Date:** 2026-05-20  
**Status:** follow-on outcome artifact  
**Primary scope:** `Loep Culture Assessment / Loep Cultuurbeeld`  
**Canonical route id:** `culture_assessment`

---

## 1. Purpose

This register defines the bounded set of allowed post-baseline outcomes for `culture_assessment`.

No additional route outcome may be invented locally.

---

## 2. Canonical Outcomes

### 2.1 No immediate next route

Use when:

- the baseline question has been answered well enough for now
- board and HR can proceed with bounded action without another measurement route
- no governance-safe re-measurement question exists yet

### 2.2 Deeper governed work

Use when:

- the organization needs more structured interpretation before another measurement route is justified
- HR or sponsors need governed analysis, alignment, or cascade preparation
- the right next step is still interpretive rather than measurement-led

### 2.3 Pulse follow-on

Use when:

- the deterministic Pulse eligibility rule passes
- one bounded theme deserves structured re-checking
- the route remains explicitly follow-on and does not replace the annual baseline

### 2.4 Another Loep route

Use when:

- the next useful question is no longer broad annual culture and engagement
- the baseline has surfaced a narrower route fit

Allowed adjacent routes:

- `RetentieScan`
- `ExitScan`

---

## 3. No-Next-Route Guardrail

`No immediate next route` is a valid and supported outcome.

It may not be framed as:

- a failed sale
- a missing upsell
- a temporary placeholder until `Pulse` is sold anyway

---

## 4. Deeper-Governed-Work Guardrail

`Deeper governed work` is distinct from route switching.

It may include:

- governed HR interpretation
- bounded segment reading
- board-to-HR alignment
- cascade preparation

It may not be reframed as:

- a hidden analyst sandbox
- an unbounded consulting layer
- a default step toward another product

---

## 5. Approval Read

This register is acceptable only when:

- all four outcomes are explicit
- no other outcome is implied
- no-next-route remains supported
- deeper governed work remains distinct from measurement-led follow-on
