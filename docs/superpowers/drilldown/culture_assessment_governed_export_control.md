# Loep Culture Assessment Governed Export Control

**Date:** 2026-05-20  
**Status:** governed export control artifact  
**Primary scope:** `Loep Culture Assessment / Loep Cultuurbeeld`  
**Canonical route id:** `culture_assessment`

---

## 1. Export Rule

Exports are stricter than views.

No export may occur unless:

- the layer is already release-safe
- the role is export-entitled
- package scope includes the governed output
- governance owner or delegated approval path exists

---

## 2. Always Forbidden

- respondent-level export
- raw local score export below threshold
- manager-limited export of drilldown layers
- export of hidden suppressed layer data

---

## 3. Governed-Only Exports

Allowed only where explicitly entitled:

- segment summary export
- HR appendix export
- governed comparison summary

---

## 4. Ownership

Must be explicit for:

- who may approve export
- who may download export
- who reviews denied-export disputes

---

## 5. Approval

Approved when:

- forbidden export paths are explicit
- governed export classes are explicit
- approval ownership is explicit
