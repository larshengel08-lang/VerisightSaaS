---

## 1. Purpose

This note defines what HR can do in the governed analysis layer without turning the route into a free-form analytics workspace.

---

## 2. HR Layer May Provide

- safe segment layer overview
- suppression and hidden-reason visibility
- governed comparison summary
- export entitlement status
- follow-up boundaries and escalation cues

---

## 3. HR Layer May Not Provide

- infinite slicing
- free-form query behavior
- silent hidden-layer overrides
- raw quote browsing
- local blame narratives

---

## 4. Follow-up Boundary

The HR layer may support:

- governed interpretation
- safe follow-up framing
- escalation toward delivery or governance owner

It may not support:

- unsupported causal diagnosis
- local blame assignment
- manager ranking behavior

---

## 5. Approval

Approved when:

- allowed HR depth is explicit
- prohibited analytics behavior is explicit
- follow-up boundary is explicit
