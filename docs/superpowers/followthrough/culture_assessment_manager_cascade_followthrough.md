# Loep Culture Assessment Manager Cascade Follow-through

**Date:** 2026-05-20  
**Status:** follow-through cascade artifact  
**Primary scope:** `Loep Culture Assessment / Loep Cultuurbeeld`  
**Canonical route id:** `culture_assessment`

---

## 1. Purpose

This artifact defines what safe local follow-through can look like for managers.

It exists to keep cascade behavior:

- communication-oriented
- non-ranking
- non-disciplinary

---

## 2. Communication Prompts

Managers may use prompts such as:

- what did you recognize in the organization-level read?
- what feels relevant for our team to discuss safely?
- what questions should we escalate instead of trying to answer locally?
- what should we avoid concluding from this baseline?

---

## 3. Prohibited Local Inference

Managers may not infer:

- that their team is being ranked
- that a low local pattern proves poor management
- that individual employees are identifiable from this route
- that local action is justified beyond visible governance boundaries

---

## 4. Cascade Boundary

Manager follow-through is allowed only as:

- communication support
- question collection
- escalation into HR or route governance when needed

It is not allowed as:

- score defense
- performance management
- local blame allocation

---

## 5. Approval

Approved when:

- communication prompts are explicit
- prohibited local inference is explicit
- cascade boundary is explicit
