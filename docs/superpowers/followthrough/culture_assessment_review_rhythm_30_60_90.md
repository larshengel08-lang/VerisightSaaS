# Loep Culture Assessment Review Rhythm 30 60 90

**Date:** 2026-05-20  
**Status:** follow-through rhythm artifact  
**Primary scope:** `Loep Culture Assessment / Loep Cultuurbeeld`  
**Canonical route id:** `culture_assessment`

---

## 1. Purpose

This artifact defines the bounded review rhythm after the baseline.

---

## 2. 30-Day Review

Artifact:

- `30-day board/HR follow-up confirmation note`

Purpose:

- confirm what is actually being followed up
- confirm what remains hypothesis rather than conclusion
- prevent unsupported action drift

---

## 3. 60-Day Review

Artifact:

- `60-day governance drift check and cascade questions log`

Purpose:

- check whether follow-up remains bounded and useful
- review whether cascade questions are staying within governance
- identify any drift toward ranking, causal overclaim, or local blame

---

## 4. 90-Day Review

Artifact:

- `90-day route decision note`

Purpose:

- determine whether no immediate next route, deeper governed work, or a bounded follow-on route is appropriate

---

## 5. Approval

Approved when:

- all three review points are explicit
- each review point has a clear purpose
- governance drift checks are explicit
