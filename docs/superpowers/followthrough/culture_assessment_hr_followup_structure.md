# Loep Culture Assessment HR Follow-up Structure

**Date:** 2026-05-20  
**Status:** follow-through HR artifact  
**Primary scope:** `Loep Culture Assessment / Loep Cultuurbeeld`  
**Canonical route id:** `culture_assessment`

---

## 1. Purpose

This artifact defines how HR structures follow-up after the executive read.

---

## 2. Allowed HR Follow-up Types

HR may:

- define focus areas for governed discussion
- plan who needs to be involved in deeper interpretation
- determine which deeper outputs are safe and useful
- clarify how local conversations should be framed
- clarify whether a 30/60/90 review rhythm is sufficient without new measurement

---

## 3. Forbidden HR Follow-up Types

HR may not:

- turn the route into a free-form analytics program without governance
- back-solve causality from one baseline
- use the route as a disciplinary instrument
- mandate intervention recipes from one baseline read
- use unclear local signals as manager-performance evidence

---

## 4. Escalation Boundary

Escalation is appropriate when:

- governance disputes remain unresolved
- a hidden layer is being pressured for visibility
- follow-up questions exceed safe local visibility
- Pulse is being requested to compensate for unclear baseline interpretation

---

## 5. Approval

Approved when:

- allowed HR follow-up types are explicit
- forbidden HR follow-up types are explicit
- escalation boundary is explicit
