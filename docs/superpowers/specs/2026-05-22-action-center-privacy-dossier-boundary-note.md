# Action Center Privacy And Dossier Boundary Note

## Audience

Legal/privacy reviewers, HR buyers, HR operators, IT/security reviewers

## Purpose

Define what Action Center inside Loep stores, what it must never become, and how bounded follow-through stays separate from broad employee-record or surveillance behavior.

## Acceptance Test

A reviewer can point to explicit prohibited uses, explicit allowed content, and a clear rule that keeps Action Center outside dossier-style, surveillance, and risk-ledger framing.

## Sign-Off Owner

Primary: privacy/legal reviewer
Fallback: founder/product owner if no separate privacy/legal reviewer is assigned

## What Action Center Is Not

- not a personnel dossier
- not employee monitoring
- not an employee risk ledger
- not a broad case file about an employee
- not a narrative store for performance judgment

## What Action Center Does Store

- route-bound follow-through state after an approved scan
- bounded action history tied to that route
- review history showing whether follow-through happened, stalled, or needs continuation
- governance state tied to continuation, escalation, and closeout

## What Does Not Belong

- open-ended employee narratives unrelated to route follow-through
- surveillance commentary or observational logging about employee behavior
- individual risk scoring, prediction, or judgment
- broad case histories that try to turn Action Center into an HR record system
- unrelated performance documentation, disciplinary history, or dossier-style notes

## Boundary Rule

Action Center records bounded post-scan follow-through inside Loep. It does not judge individual employees, does not maintain dossier-like histories, and does not turn governance readback into employee monitoring.

## Why This Boundary Matters

- it keeps follow-through evidence tied to a route instead of drifting into broad employee history
- it keeps HR governance explicit without widening Action Center into a broader operating record
- it protects buyer, operator, and reviewer understanding of what Action Center is for

## Plain-Language Summary

Action Center stores the minimum bounded history needed to govern route follow-through. It is not a dossier, not a monitoring surface, and not an employee risk record.
