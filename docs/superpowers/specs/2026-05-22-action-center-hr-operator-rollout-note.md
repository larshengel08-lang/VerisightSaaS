# Action Center HR Operator Rollout Note

## Audience

HR operators responsible for rollout and day-to-day use

## Purpose

Explain how HR should roll out and operate Action Center inside Loep without widening it into broad route administration, workflow ownership, or open-ended employee recordkeeping.

## Acceptance Test

An HR operator can explain how to use Action Center day to day, how HR governance fits, and what not to add or promise during rollout.

## Sign-Off Owner

Primary: governance/trust reviewer
Fallback: founder/product owner if no separate governance/trust reviewer is assigned

## Start Here

- Use Action Center as the route-bound follow-through layer after an approved scan.
- Keep work tied to approved route families only: `exit` and `retention`.
- Treat route, action, review, continuation, and closeout as the operating grammar.
- Keep Action Center as the system of record for follow-through state inside Loep.

## What HR Owns

- the operating rhythm around active routes
- interpretation of review outcomes and follow-through pressure
- continuation, escalation, reopen, and closeout decisions
- keeping Action Center bounded to post-scan follow-through

## What To Watch During Rollout

- stalled actions or review backlog
- repeated no-progress loops that need intervention
- route sprawl that weakens closeout discipline
- signs that participants are treating Action Center like a task board or case file

## What To Reinforce With Participants

- managers participate through bounded route actions and reviews
- action completion alone does not close a route
- HR remains the governance owner for continuation and closeout
- follow-through visibility is not the same as proof of intervention impact

## What Not To Turn This Into

- not a case-management system
- not a task tracker or project board
- not workflow software
- not a personnel dossier or broad employee record

## Rollout Rule

If a rollout explanation only works by making Action Center sound broader than route-bound follow-through, stop and tighten the framing before rollout continues.

## Plain-Language Summary

HR uses Action Center to govern route-bound follow-through inside Loep. Keep the operating rhythm clear, keep boundaries intact, and do not widen the product during rollout.
