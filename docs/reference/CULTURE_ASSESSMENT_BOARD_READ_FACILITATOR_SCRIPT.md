# Loep Culture Assessment Board-Read Facilitator Script

## Duration
60-90 minutes

## Goal
Guide a board-level read of the annual culture and engagement baseline without falling into free score interpretation, manager ranking or causal diagnosis.

## Agenda
1. Scope and governance reminder
2. Response basis and coverage
3. Loep Culture Index as navigation signal
4. Domain picture
5. 3-5 board attention points
6. Safe segment contrasts
7. Decision questions
8. Follow-on route choice

## Facilitator Guardrails
- keep language descriptive
- do not turn the index into a health verdict
- do not speculate about individual managers
- do not promise cause-and-effect
