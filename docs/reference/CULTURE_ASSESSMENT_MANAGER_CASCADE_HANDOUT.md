# Loep Culture Assessment Manager Cascade Handout

## This Handout May
- explain what was measured at organization level
- support safe team communication
- structure a follow-up conversation without score defense

## This Handout May Not
- compare teams as a ranking
- ask managers to defend low scores
- discuss individual signals
- show local scores below threshold

## Manager Message
Start from broad organization patterns, then ask what local context might help explain them. Stay descriptive and avoid blame.

## Sample cascade-safe page: Noordhaven Industrie Groep

Page title:
- `Hoe bespreek je de board-baseline veilig met teams`

Page may include:
- what the organization-level baseline looked at
- what managers may say in a team conversation
- what questions help collect local context without score defense

Suggested manager prompts:
- `Welke brede patronen herken je wel of niet in de dagelijkse praktijk?`
- `Welke context helpt om richting, werkdruk of ontwikkeling beter te begrijpen?`
- `Welke signalen verdienen eerst verduidelijking voordat we vervolgkeuzes maken?`

Page may not include:
- team ranking
- manager comparison
- hidden segment data
- prompts that ask a manager to defend a low score
